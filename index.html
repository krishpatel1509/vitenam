Fansipan Peak · Glass Bridge · Heaven Gate · Café Moana

1 Night · Day 3  |  Vietnam's Cultural Capital
DAY 3
Sapa → Hanoi · Temple of Literature · Hoan Kiem · Old Quarter Cyclo
City Immersion
BreakfastDinner

Check out and descend back to Hanoi. Discover the Temple of Literature (Văn Miếu, founded 1070) — Vietnam's first university with pavilions, lotus ponds, and ancient doctoral stelae. Stroll Hoan Kiem Lake, cross the red Huc Bridge to Ngoc Son Temple. Finish with a 30-min Old Quarter Cyclo Tour weaving through 36 ancient trade streets. Spot iconic Train Street en route. Dinner at Indian restaurant.

Destination 03
Hạ Long Bay
1 Night Cruise · Days 4–5  |  UNESCO World Heritage Site
DAY 4
Hạ Long Bay Overnight Cruise · Sung Sot Cave · Ti Top Island · Luon Cave
Overnight Cruise
BreakfastDinner on Board

Check out from Hanoi and drive ~3.5 hrs to the UNESCO World Heritage Site. Board your overnight junk cruise and sail through emerald waters studded with 1,969 limestone karsts. Explore cathedral-like Sung Sot (Surprise) Cave, kayak silently through the hidden lagoon of Luon Cave, and hike to the summit of Ti Top Island for a 360° panorama. Watch the sunset gild the limestone pillars, then dine on fresh seafood under the stars.

🛳️ Overnight Junk Cruise🗿 Sung Sot Cave🚣 Luon Cave Kayak🏝️ Ti Top Island Hike🌅 Bay Sunset🦞 Seafood Dinner on Deck

Destination 04
Đà Nẵng & Hội An
3 Nights · Days 5–8  |  Beach · Hills · Ancient Lantern Town
DAY 6
Bà Nà Hills · Golden Bridge · Fantasy Park · French Village
Theme Park & Panoramas
BreakfastLunchDinner

Board the record-breaking cable car up to Bà Nà Hills at 1,489 m. Walk the world-famous Golden Bridge — held aloft by two giant mossy stone hands — for breathtaking views over jungle and sea. Explore the enchanting mock French Village with Romanesque clock towers and flower gardens. Enjoy unlimited rides at massive indoor Fantasy Park. Visit sacred Linh Chua Linh Tu Temple before descending back to Đà Nẵng.

🚠 Bà Nà Hills Cable Car🌉 Golden Bridge🏰 French Village🎢 Fantasy Park⛩️ Linh Chua Linh Tu
DAY 7
Sơn Trà · Lady Buddha · Marble Mountain · Hội An Ancient Town · Coconut Forest
Culture & Lanterns
BreakfastDinner

Start at Sơn Trà Peninsula visiting the 67 m Lady Buddha at Linh Ứng Pagoda. Stop at Marble Mountain — five limestone peaks with caves and temples. Drive south to Hội An: first spin through Bay Mau Coconut Forest in a traditional round basket boat. Then enter glowing Hội An Ancient Town — cross the centuries-old Japanese Covered Bridge, browse silk lantern galleries, and lose yourself in the Hội An Night Market. Check for 🏮 Lantern Festival dates below!

DAY 9
Grand World · VinWonders · Sunset Town
Entertainment Paradise
BreakfastDinner

Explore Grand World Phú Quốc — a Venice-inspired entertainment city with gondola canals, international food lanes, cultural performances, and dazzling nightly light shows. Vinpearl Safari · Thom Island · Sunset Sanato Beach Club

BreakfastDinner

Return for golden-hour cocktails and a spectacular beachside dinner at Sunset Sanato Beach Club.

Before You Go

Essential Tips

📋
Visa
Indian passport holders apply for Vietnam e-Visa online at evisa.xuatnhapcanh.gov.vn. Valid 90 days, single or multiple entry. Apply 5–7 days before departure.
🪙
Currency
Vietnamese Đồng (VND). ₹1 ≈ 290 VND. ATMs everywhere. USD accepted at major resorts. Carry cash for local markets.
🌡️
Weather in June
Sapa: cool 15–22°C — pack a light jacket. Hanoi: hot & humid 32°C. Da Nang: hot 33°C, brief showers. Phu Quoc: wet season starts but mornings are usually sunny.
🎆
Fireworks Festival
Da Nang Fireworks: June 13, 20, 27 + grand finale June 28. Book Han River-view spots or arrive 2 hrs early. Dragon Bridge fire: every Sat & Sun 9 PM.
🏮
Hội An Lantern Festival
Full moon festival: May 30, June 13, 20, 27, 28. Ancient Town lights off — silk lanterns float down the Thu Bon River. Unmissable.
🧳
Packing
Light cotton clothes + warm layer for Sapa. Comfortable walking shoes, SPF 50+ sunscreen, mosquito repellent, small day-pack, universal adapter (Type A/C).
✈️
Domestic Flights
Book Hanoi→Da Nang and Da Nang→Phu Quoc with VietJet, Vietnam Airlines, or Bamboo Airways. Book 4–6 weeks ahead for best prices.
📱
SIM Card
Pick up a Viettel or Vietnamobile SIM at Hanoi Airport — ~₹350 for 10 GB/30 days. Excellent 4G even in Sapa highlands.
